About us

Alba Bau + GmbH is a contractor for various projects in the field of construction. Our company has a long history as a construction company and has proven itself to be a reliable partner in the execution of shell construction projects on the German market since 2011. We offer exemplary quality and unbeatable prices when carrying out the commissioned work.
